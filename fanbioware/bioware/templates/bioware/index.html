About us

Look deeper into BioWare and our history.

  • Foundation
    BioWare was founded in 1995. So we are 26 years in the gaming industry.
  • What we do
    We create high-quality console, PC games and specialize in role-playing games.
  • Our projects
    We developed 20 games focused on rich stories, unforgettable characters, and vast worlds to discover.

Our games

Please explore our most popular games. We invite you to join us on the journey.

  • Mass effect
    The galaxy is trapped in an endless cycle of extinction. Every 50,000 years, an ancient machine race invades with ruthless efficiency, wiping out all advanced organic civilization.
  • MASS EFFECT 2
    Two years after Commander Shepard repelled the invading Reapers bent on the destruction of organic life, a mysterious new enemy emerges.
  • DRAGON AGE: ORIGINS
    Dragon Age: Origins is an epic tale of violence, passion, and betrayal. A Blight threatens to destroy the world, and survival rests in the hands of those chosen by fate.
  • DRAGON AGE II
    Experience the epic sequel to the 2009 Game of the Year. In Dragon Age II, you are one of the few who escaped the destruction of your home.
  • DRAGON AGE: INQUISITION
    Lead the Inquisition to hunt down the agents of chaos. The choices you make will forever change the Dragon Age.
